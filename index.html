Visit CDMX

Discover a fusion of ancient heritage and contemporary soul in the continent's most vibrant metropolis.

Top three activities to do at CDMX

Explore the Floating Gardens of Xochimilco.

Hop on a colorful trajinera boat to experience an ancient Aztec canal system that remains the city's favorite weekend party spot.

Marvel at the Palacio de Bellas Artes.

Step inside this marble masterpiece to see world-famous revolutionary murals housed within a stunning, gold-leafed Art Deco interior.

Wander through Chapultepec Park.

Discover a historic castle overlooking the city, surrounded by contemporary art museums and lush forests in one of the world's oldest urban parks.
